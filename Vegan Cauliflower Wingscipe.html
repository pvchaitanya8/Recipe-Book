Vegan Cauliflower Wingscipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium cauliflower head, cut into florets
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (or gluten-free flour for a gluten-free version)
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk (or any non-dairy milk)
  • 1 tablespoon ground flaxseed mixed with 3 tablespoons of water (flax egg substitute)
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

For the sauce:

  • 1/2 cup hot sauce (such as Frank's RedHot or any other vegan brand)
  • 1/4 cup melted vegan butter or olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup or agave nectar (adjust to taste)

For the coating (choose one option):

  • 1 cup breadcrumbs (use gluten-free breadcrumbs for a gluten-free version)
  • OR 1 cup panko breadcrumbs (use gluten-free panko for a gluten-free version)
  • OR 1 cup almond flour for a grain-free option

Optional garnish:

  • Fresh chopped parsley or cilantro
  • Vegan ranch dressing or vegan blue cheese dressing for dipping

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it to prevent sticking.
  2. Prepare the flax egg: In a small bowl, mix the ground flaxseed with water and let it sit for about 5 minutes until it thickens and resembles the texture of an egg.
  3. Prepare the batter: In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, almond milk, prepared flax egg, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper until you get a smooth batter.
  4. Coat the cauliflower: Dip each cauliflower floret into the batter, making sure to coat it evenly. Allow any excess batter to drip off before proceeding to the next step.
  5. Choose the coating: Roll each cauliflower-coated floret in your chosen coating (breadcrumbs, panko, or almond flour), pressing gently to ensure the coating adheres well. Place the coated cauliflower wings onto the prepared baking sheet in a single layer.
  6. Bake the cauliflower wings: Bake the cauliflower in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes or until they are crispy and golden brown, flipping them halfway through to ensure even cooking.
  7. Prepare the sauce: While the cauliflower wings are baking, in a separate bowl, mix the hot sauce, melted vegan butter or olive oil, and maple syrup or agave nectar to make the spicy glaze.
  8. Toss the wings in the sauce: Once the cauliflower wings are done baking, transfer them to a large mixing bowl and pour the sauce over them. Toss the wings gently until they are evenly coated with the spicy glaze.
  9. Serve: Garnish the vegan cauliflower wings with chopped parsley or cilantro if desired. Serve them hot with vegan ranch dressing or vegan blue cheese dressing on the side for dipping.

Enjoy your delicious and cruelty-free Vegan Cauliflower Wings as a tasty appetizer or party snack!
