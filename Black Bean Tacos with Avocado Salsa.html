Black Bean Tacos with Avocado Salsa

Sure, here's a preparation method for making delicious Black Bean Tacos with Avocado Salsa:

Ingredients:

For Black Bean Tacos:

  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 small tortillas (corn or flour)

For Avocado Salsa:

  • 2 ripe avocados, diced
  • 1 small tomato, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 jalapeño, seeded and finely chopped (optional for heat)
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Optional toppings:

  • Shredded lettuce or cabbage
  • Grated cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, or vegan cheese)
  • Sour cream or Greek yogurt (or a dairy-free alternative)
  • Hot sauce or salsa

Instructions:

1. Prepare the Avocado Salsa:

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the diced avocados, tomatoes, red onion, jalapeño (if using), lime juice, and chopped cilantro.
  2. Gently toss the ingredients together until well mixed.
  3. Season the salsa with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap, ensuring it touches the surface of the salsa to prevent browning, and refrigerate until ready to serve.

2. Make the Black Bean Filling:

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the finely chopped onion and sauté for about 2-3 minutes until it becomes translucent.
  3. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for an additional 1 minute until fragrant.

3. Prepare the Black Beans:

  1. Add the drained and rinsed black beans to the skillet with the sautéed onions and garlic.
  2. Sprinkle the ground cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper over the beans.
  3. Stir everything together, allowing the beans to heat through and absorb the flavors. Cook for about 5-7 minutes on medium-low heat.

4. Warm the Tortillas:

  1. While the black bean mixture is cooking, warm the tortillas on a dry skillet over medium heat for about 10-15 seconds on each side. Alternatively, you can wrap them in a damp paper towel and microwave for a few seconds until they are soft and pliable.

5. Assemble the Black Bean Tacos:

  1. Spoon the black bean filling onto each warm tortilla.
  2. Top the tacos with the prepared Avocado Salsa and any optional toppings you desire, such as shredded lettuce or cabbage, grated cheese, sour cream, or hot sauce.

6. Serve and Enjoy:

  1. Arrange the prepared Black Bean Tacos on a serving platter.
  2. Serve immediately while they are still warm, allowing everyone to customize their tacos with additional toppings as desired.

These Black Bean Tacos with Avocado Salsa are a delicious and satisfying vegetarian option for lunch or dinner. Enjoy!
