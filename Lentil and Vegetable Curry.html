Lentil and Vegetable Curry

A delicious and nutritious dish that's easy to prepare. Here's a step-by-step preparation method for making this flavorful curry:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried lentils (red or green), rinsed and soaked overnight (optional, but reduces cooking time)
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil (vegetable oil, coconut oil, or ghee)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 large tomatoes, diced
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (e.g., carrots, bell peppers, cauliflower, zucchini), chopped
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 2 cups vegetable broth or water
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional, for heat)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro (coriander leaves), for garnish
  • Cooked rice or naan bread, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Rinse and soak the lentils (if using) in water overnight. This step can help reduce the cooking time, but it's optional. If you're short on time, you can skip soaking and just rinse the lentils.
  2. Heat the cooking oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat.
  3. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until they become soft and translucent, about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Stir in the minced garlic and grated ginger, and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  5. Add the diced tomatoes to the pot and cook until they start to soften and release their juices, about 3-4 minutes.
  6. Now, it's time to add the mixed vegetables. Stir them into the onion-tomato mixture and cook for a few minutes until they slightly soften.
  7. Drain the soaked lentils (if using) and add them to the pot. If you didn't soak the lentils, rinse them and add them directly to the pot.
  8. Pour in the coconut milk and vegetable broth or water. Stir everything together.
  9. Add the curry powder, ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric powder, cayenne pepper (if using), and salt. Mix well to ensure all the ingredients are evenly coated in the spices.
  10. Bring the mixture to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let it cook for about 20-25 minutes. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking and to ensure even cooking.
  11. Check the lentils and vegetables for tenderness. If they're fully cooked and tender, your curry is ready. If needed, you can cook for a few more minutes until everything is perfectly done.
  12. Taste the curry and adjust the seasoning according to your preference.
  13. Once the lentils and vegetables are cooked to your liking, turn off the heat.
  14. Garnish the Lentil and Vegetable Curry with fresh cilantro (coriander leaves) before serving.
  15. Serve the curry hot with cooked rice or naan bread on the side.

Enjoy your homemade Lentil and Vegetable Curry! It's a delightful and hearty meal that's perfect for vegetarians and anyone looking for a healthy and flavorful dish.
