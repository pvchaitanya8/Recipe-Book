Bean and Rice Burritos with Salsa

Black Bean Burritos

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 1 1/4 cups salsa, divided
  • 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese, divided
  • 4 large whole wheat flour tortillas

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the rice, 1 cup of the salsa, and 2 cups of water. Bring to a boil over high heat, stirring twice. Cover, reduce heat to low, and simmer for 20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the liquid is almost absorbed.
  2. Stir in the beans and half of the cheddar cheese. Let stand, covered, for 2 minutes.
  3. To assemble the burritos, spread 1/4 of the bean mixture down the center of each tortilla. Top with the remaining salsa and cheese. Fold up the bottom of the tortilla, then fold in the sides and roll up tightly.
  4. Serve immediately, or wrap in foil and refrigerate for later.

Tips:

  • For a spicier burrito, add a tablespoon of chili powder to the bean mixture.
  • For a more authentic flavor, use corn tortillas instead of flour tortillas.
  • Top your burritos with your favorite toppings, such as sour cream, guacamole, or sliced avocado.

Cooking time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4
