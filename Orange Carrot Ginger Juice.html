Orange Carrot Ginger Juice

Ingredients:

  • 3 large oranges, peeled and halved
  • 4 large carrots, chopped
  • 1 (1-inch) piece fresh ginger, peeled and grated
  • Optional: 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Ice cubes, for serving

Instructions:

  1. If using a juicer, juice the oranges, carrots, and ginger.
  2. If using a blender, combine the oranges, carrots, and ginger in the blender and blend until smooth.
  3. Strain the juice through a fine-mesh sieve or nut milk bag to remove any pulp.
  4. Stir in the lemon juice, if desired.
  5. Pour the juice over ice and serve immediately.

Tips:

  • For a sweeter juice, you can add more oranges or a teaspoon of honey.
  • If you don't have a juicer, you can grate the carrots and ginger and then blend them with the oranges.
  • This juice is best served immediately, but it can also be stored in the refrigerator for up to 24 hours.

Health Benefits:

  • Orange Carrot Ginger Juice is a great source of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants.
  • It's also a great way to boost your immune system and fight off infection.
  • Carrots are an excellent source of beta-carotene (vitamin A), essential for good vision, healthy skin, and a robust immune system.
  • Oranges are a good source of vitamin C, which is also important for immune function.
  • Ginger is a natural anti-inflammatory and can help to relieve nausea and stomach upset.

Enjoy!
