Tuna Salad Sandwiches

Ingredients:

  • 2 (6-ounce) cans tuna, drained
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup chopped celery
  • 2 tablespoons chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  • 8 slices bread

Instructions:

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the tuna, mayonnaise, celery, onion, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Stir until well combined.
  2. Spread the tuna salad on the bread slices.
  3. Serve immediately or chill for later.

Tips:

  • For a more flavorful tuna salad, add a teaspoon of Dijon mustard or a tablespoon of capers.
  • If you want a lighter tuna salad, use low-fat mayonnaise or Greek yogurt.
  • To make your tuna salad extra creamy, mash the tuna with a fork before adding the mayonnaise.
  • For a fun twist, try adding some chopped hard-boiled eggs, chopped pickles, or chopped carrots to your tuna salad.

Condiments:

  • You can serve your tuna salad sandwiches with your favorite condiments, such as ketchup, mustard, or hot sauce.
  • You can also add a slice of cheese to your sandwich for a more substantial meal.
  • If you're looking for a healthier option, you can serve your tuna salad on lettuce leaves or whole-wheat bread.

Enjoy!
