Rich and Fudgy Brownies

Preparation Method for Rich and Fudgy Brownies:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips or chunks (optional, but highly recommended)
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts (e.g., walnuts or pecans) (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan and line it with parchment paper, leaving some overhang on the sides for easy removal.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter until it's completely liquefied. You can also melt it on the stovetop over low heat. Let the melted butter cool slightly.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the melted butter and granulated sugar until well combined.
  4. Add the eggs, one at a time, to the butter-sugar mixture, whisking well after each addition. Make sure the eggs are fully incorporated.
  5. Stir in the vanilla extract to the mixture and mix until smooth.
  6. In a separate bowl, sift the all-purpose flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt. If you don't have a sifter, you can whisk the dry ingredients together to remove any lumps.
  7. Gradually add the dry ingredient mixture to the wet ingredients, folding gently with a spatula until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; you want a fudgy texture, not a cakey one.
  8. If you desire extra chocolatey goodness, fold in the chocolate chips or chunks into the batter. You can also add chopped nuts for a crunchy texture and nutty flavor.
  9. Pour the brownie batter into the prepared baking pan, spreading it out evenly with a spatula.
  10. Bake the brownies in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs. Remember, you want them to be fudgy, so be cautious not to overbake.
  11. Once done, remove the pan from the oven and let the brownies cool completely in the pan on a wire rack.
  12. Once cooled, lift the brownies from the pan using the parchment paper overhang and transfer them to a cutting board.
  13. Cut the brownies into squares or rectangles of your desired size. Clean the knife between cuts for neater edges.
  14. Serve the rich and fudgy brownies on their own or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an extra indulgence!

These brownies are perfect for satisfying any chocolate cravings or for sharing with friends and family on special occasions. Enjoy!
