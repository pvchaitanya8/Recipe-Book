Grilled Chicken with Steamed Broccoli and Brown Rice

A delicious and healthy meal. Here's a step-by-step preparation method to make this dish:

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges (for serving, optional)

Preparation:

1. Marinate the Chicken:

  • In a mixing bowl, combine 1 tablespoon olive oil, minced garlic, paprika, dried thyme, salt, and black pepper.
  • Add the chicken breasts to the bowl and coat them thoroughly with the marinade.
  • Cover the bowl and let the chicken marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes. For best results, you can marinate it overnight.

2. Cook Brown Rice:

  • Rinse the brown rice in cold water until the water runs clear.
  • In a medium-sized saucepan, bring 2 cups of water to a boil.
  • Add the rinsed brown rice to the boiling water and stir.
  • Reduce the heat to low, cover the saucepan with a lid, and let the rice simmer for about 40-45 minutes or until the rice is tender and the water is absorbed.
  • Fluff the rice with a fork and keep it warm while you prepare the chicken and broccoli.

3. Grill the Chicken:

  • Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. You can also use a grill pan on the stovetop if you don't have an outdoor grill.
  • Remove the marinated chicken breasts from the bowl and let any excess marinade drip off.
  • Brush the grill grates with a little oil to prevent sticking.
  • Place the chicken breasts on the grill and cook for about 5-6 minutes per side or until the chicken is cooked through and reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C). The cooking time may vary depending on the thickness of the chicken breasts.

4. Steam the Broccoli:

  • While the chicken is grilling, steam the broccoli.
  • Fill a large pot with about an inch of water and bring it to a boil.
  • Place a steamer basket in the pot and add the broccoli florets to the basket.
  • Cover the pot with a lid and steam the broccoli for about 3-5 minutes, or until the broccoli is tender-crisp and bright green.

5. Serve:

  • Once the chicken is grilled to perfection, remove it from the grill and let it rest for a couple of minutes before slicing.
  • Divide the cooked brown rice among serving plates and top with sliced grilled chicken and steamed broccoli.
  • You can drizzle a little extra olive oil over the chicken and broccoli for added flavor.
  • Serve the Grilled Chicken with Steamed Broccoli and Brown Rice with lemon wedges on the side for an extra burst of freshness (optional).

Enjoy your nutritious and delicious Grilled Chicken with Steamed Broccoli and Brown Rice!
