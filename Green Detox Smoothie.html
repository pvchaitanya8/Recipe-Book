Green Detox Smoothie

Green Detox Smoothie

This smoothie is a great way to start your day or as a snack. It's packed with nutrients and is a delicious way to detox your body.

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 cup baby spinach
    • 1 cup kale
    • 1/2 cup frozen pineapple chunks
    • 1/2 cup frozen banana
    • 1/2 inch ginger, peeled and grated
    • 1/4 cup water or almond milk
    • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
    • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Instructions:
    • Combine all of the ingredients in a blender.
    • Blend until smooth and creamy.
    • Pour into a glass and enjoy!
  • Tips:
    • You can adjust the amount of each ingredient to your liking.
    • If you don't have frozen pineapple, you can use fresh pineapple, but you may need to add more water or almond milk to the blender.
    • You can also add other fruits or vegetables to this smoothie, such as berries, celery, or cucumber.
  • Nutritional Information:
    • Calories: 250
    • Fat: 8 grams
    • Saturated Fat: 1 gram
    • Carbohydrates: 40 grams
    • Fiber: 10 grams
    • Sugar: 20 grams
    • Protein: 5 grams
  • Health Benefits:
    • The spinach and kale in this smoothie are packed with vitamins A, C, and K, as well as other important nutrients.
    • The pineapple is a good source of vitamin C and manganese.
    • The ginger helps to boost your immune system and can help to reduce inflammation.
    • The chia seeds are a good source of fiber and omega-3 fatty acids.
    • The lemon juice adds a tart flavor and also helps to boost your immune system.
