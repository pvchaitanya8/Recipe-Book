Apple Cinnamon Crisp

Apple Cinnamon Crisp is a delightful dessert that combines the sweet and tart flavors of apples with the warm spice of cinnamon, all topped with a crunchy, buttery crumble. Here's a step-by-step preparation method for making Apple Cinnamon Crisp:

Ingredients:

For the Filling:

  • 6 cups of peeled and sliced apples (such as Granny Smith, Honeycrisp, or a mix)
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

For the Crumble Topping:

  • 1 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup cold unsalted butter, cut into small cubes

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking dish with butter or non-stick cooking spray.
  2. Prepare the apple filling: In a large bowl, combine the sliced apples, granulated sugar, lemon juice, ground cinnamon, ground nutmeg, and all-purpose flour. Mix well until all the apples are coated evenly.
  3. Transfer the apple filling: Spread the coated apple slices evenly in the greased baking dish, making sure to distribute them in a single layer.
  4. Prepare the crumble topping: In another bowl, combine the rolled oats, all-purpose flour, brown sugar, ground cinnamon, and salt. Mix well. Then, add the cold cubed butter to the mixture.
  5. Crumble the topping: Using your fingers or a pastry cutter, work the cold butter into the dry mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs and the butter is well incorporated. This will create the crumbly texture for the topping.
  6. Top the apples: Evenly distribute the crumble topping over the apple filling in the baking dish.
  7. Bake the crisp: Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for about 40-45 minutes or until the topping turns golden brown, and the apple filling is bubbly and tender.
  8. Cool and serve: Once the Apple Cinnamon Crisp is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool slightly before serving. You can enjoy it warm as is or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream for added indulgence.
  9. Store leftovers: If you have any leftovers, you can store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator for a few days. Reheat in the oven before serving to regain the crisp texture.

Enjoy your delicious Apple Cinnamon Crisp! It's a perfect dessert for any time of the year, but it's especially comforting during the fall season.
