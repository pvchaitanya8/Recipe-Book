Zucchini Noodles with Avocado Pesto Sauce

A delicious and healthy alternative to traditional pasta dishes. Here's a step-by-step preparation method to make this refreshing meal:

Ingredients:

  • 4 medium zucchinis
  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts or walnuts (toasted)
  • 2-3 garlic cloves
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional, for a non-vegan version)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cherry tomatoes (optional, for garnish)
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional, for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the zucchini noodles:

    • Wash the zucchinis thoroughly and trim off both ends.
    • Using a spiralizer or julienne peeler, create long, thin noodles from the zucchinis. If you don't have a spiralizer, you can also use a vegetable peeler to make wide, flat "fettuccine-style" zucchini noodles.
    • Place the zucchini noodles in a colander, sprinkle some salt over them, and let them sit for about 15 minutes. This helps draw out excess moisture. After 15 minutes, gently pat them dry with paper towels.

  2. Prepare the avocado pesto sauce:

    • Cut the avocados in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a food processor or blender.
    • Add the fresh basil leaves, toasted pine nuts (or walnuts), garlic cloves, lemon juice, and grated Parmesan cheese (if using) to the food processor.
    • Season with salt and pepper according to your taste.
    • Blend the ingredients until you achieve a smooth and creamy consistency. While the food processor is running, drizzle in the olive oil gradually until everything is well combined.

  3. Assemble the dish:

    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the prepared zucchini noodles with the avocado pesto sauce. Toss gently until the noodles are evenly coated with the sauce.
    • If the sauce is too thick, you can add a splash of water to reach your desired consistency.
    • Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed with more salt, pepper, or lemon juice.

  4. Garnish and serve:

    • Divide the zucchini noodles into individual serving plates or bowls.
    • If desired, garnish with halved cherry tomatoes and some fresh basil leaves for extra color and flavor.
    • Serve immediately and enjoy your zucchini noodles with avocado pesto sauce while it's fresh and vibrant.

This dish is best served right after preparation to enjoy the vibrant colors and textures. It's a great option for a light lunch or dinner, and you can even add grilled chicken, shrimp, or tofu for added protein. Enjoy!
