Tropical Mango-Pineapple Smoothie

Mango Pineapple Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup frozen mango chunks
  • 1 cup frozen pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 banana, sliced
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 1/4 cup ice cubes
  • Optional: 1 teaspoon honey or agave nectar

Instructions:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into a glass and enjoy!

Tips:

  • You can use fresh or frozen mango and pineapple, but frozen fruits will give you a thicker, creamier smoothie.
  • If you don't have coconut milk, you can use any milk you like, such as almond milk, soy milk, or regular milk.
  • If you want your smoothie to be sweeter, you can add a teaspoon of honey or agave nectar.
  • Garnish with a slice of fresh mango or pineapple, if desired.

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: 250
  • Fat: 8 grams
  • Saturated Fat: 2 grams
  • Cholesterol: 0 milligrams
  • Sodium: 100 milligrams
  • Carbohydrates: 40 grams
  • Fiber: 4 grams
  • Sugar: 25 grams
  • Protein: 4 grams

Health Benefits:

  • This smoothie is a good source of vitamins C and A, which are important for immune function and eye health.
  • It is also a good source of potassium, which is important for heart health.
  • The mango and pineapple in this smoothie are both high in antioxidants, which can help protect your cells from damage.

Enjoy!
