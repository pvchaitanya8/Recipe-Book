Chickpea and Vegetable Coconut Curry

Chickpea and Vegetable Coconut Curry is a delicious and nutritious dish that combines the flavors of chickpeas, vegetables, and creamy coconut milk. Here's a step-by-step preparation method to make this flavorful curry:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried chickpeas (or 2 cans of cooked chickpeas)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 1 can (400 ml) coconut milk
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, zucchini, etc.), chopped
  • 1 cup spinach or kale, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped, for garnish
  • Cooked rice or naan bread, for serving

Instructions:

  1. If using dried chickpeas, rinse them thoroughly and soak them overnight in water. The next day, drain the soaking water and rinse the chickpeas again.
  2. If using canned chickpeas, drain and rinse them under cold water.
  3. In a large pot or deep pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and sauté until they become soft and translucent.
  4. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the pot. Sauté for another minute or until the aroma of garlic and ginger fills the air.
  5. Stir in the curry powder, ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric, and cayenne pepper. Cook the spices with the onions, garlic, and ginger for about 1-2 minutes to release their flavors.
  6. Pour in the coconut milk, and stir well to combine it with the spices and vegetables.
  7. Add the chopped mixed vegetables and chickpeas to the pot. If using dried chickpeas, ensure they are well-drained. Stir everything together.
  8. Mix in the tomato paste, which will add richness and depth to the curry.
  9. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer. Cover the pot with a lid and let it cook for about 15-20 minutes or until the vegetables are tender and the flavors have melded together.
  10. As the curry simmers, check the seasoning and add salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the cayenne pepper if you want a spicier curry.
  11. Stir in the chopped spinach or kale during the last few minutes of cooking. They will wilt and add extra nutrients to the dish.
  12. Once the vegetables are cooked to your desired tenderness, and the curry has thickened to a creamy consistency, turn off the heat.
  13. Serve the Chickpea and Vegetable Coconut Curry over cooked rice or with some warm naan bread. Garnish with fresh chopped cilantro for a burst of freshness.

Enjoy your delicious and wholesome Chickpea and Vegetable Coconut Curry!
